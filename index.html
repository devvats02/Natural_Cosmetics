VICTORIA RATMANOVA

A natural skincare specialist and spa consultant, Victoria Ratmanova has always been fascinated and inspired by the healing powers of the Earth. Her interest in herbal alchemy led to creations that work wonders on everyday skin concerns and more serious ailments. This path brought Victoria to the Natural Healing Institute in California, U.S.A., where she perfected her craft and started Naturals Cosmetics. Victoria transforms everyday, natural ingredients into luxurious skincare solutions—elixirs designed to help women of all ages find peace, harmony and health.
